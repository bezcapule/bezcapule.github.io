PROJECT

Tidy Teens is a game that involves a student trying to clean their room by clearing obstacles such as their clothes, books, or other items by placing their multi-directional soap bombs that can damage obstacles and other entities. Successfully clearing items can merit points for the player. They must successfully clear all items while avoiding entities such as mice and bugs, which can damage the player and make the player lose health. The player can also obtain power ups that can increase their clearing time or the range of their clearing. Once the player has successfully cleared all items, they may now exit the room to clear the game.
